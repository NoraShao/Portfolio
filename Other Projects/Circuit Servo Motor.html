PI Servo Motor Circuit

ENPH 253 - Electronics Laboratory: September 2023 - December 2023

Description: In this course, we built up knowledge of various electrical components, and breadboard circuit-building and debugging skills over several labs to eventually implement a circuit that used Proportional-Integral error correction to control a servo motor.

To power and probe the breadboard circuits, and perform tasks like function generation, we used an Analog Discovery 2 (AD2) digital oscilloscope and logic analyzer with a breakout board and our laptops.
